Powerful Features for Safe Renting

Explore our comprehensive suite of tools designed to protect both tenants and landlords.

AI-Powered Detection

AI Fake Listing Detector

Our advanced AI analyzes every listing in real-time, checking for manipulated images, inconsistent property details, suspicious pricing, and other red flags that indicate fraudulent listings.

  • Real-time image analysis with manipulation detection
  • Location verification using Google Maps integration
  • Price anomaly detection
  • Property detail consistency verification
  • Instant alert system for suspicious listings

Trust & Credibility

Bidirectional Trust Scores

Both tenants and landlords earn transparent trust ratings based on their rental history, payment behavior, property maintenance, and communication responsiveness. Build your reputation on Rentra.

  • Real-time trust score updates
  • Payment history tracking
  • Property care ratings from landlords
  • Communication score based on response time
  • Public profile with trust badge

Digital Documentation

Digital Rent Agreements

Generate legally compliant, digitally signed rent agreements in seconds. All contracts are stored securely, timestamped, and serve as evidence in disputes. Complies with Pakistan's IT Act 2007.

  • Pakistan-compliant legal templates
  • Digital signature support
  • Secure cloud storage with encryption
  • PDF download and print options
  • Timestamped document verification

Fair Pricing Analysis

AI Fair Rent Estimator

Machine learning-powered rent suggestions based on location, size, amenities, and market trends. Ensures both tenants and landlords get fair market rates for Pakistani properties.

  • Location-based price analysis
  • Amenity-adjusted valuations
  • Seasonal trend analysis
  • Neighborhood comparison tools
  • Historical pricing data

Secure Payments

JazzCash & EasyPaisa Payments

Secure, instant rent payments via Pakistan's leading mobile wallets. Automatic receipts, payment tracking, and proof of payment for both parties. No bank account required.

  • Instant JazzCash integration
  • EasyPaisa payment support
  • Automatic receipt generation
  • Payment history with proof
  • Scheduled payment reminders
