Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Rentra.

Is Rentra free? ▼
Rentra is completely free for tenants to browse listings, check trust scores, and apply to properties. Landlords pay a small monthly fee to list properties and access advanced features like tenant screening and AI pricing suggestions.
How does AI detect fake listings? ▼
Our AI analyzes images for manipulation, cross-references property details, checks location accuracy, and compares pricing with market trends. It flags suspicious listings instantly, protecting you from scams before you waste time.
Are digital agreements legally valid in Pakistan? ▼
Yes. Digital rent agreements on Rentra comply with Pakistan's Information Technology Act (2007) and are legally binding. They include all required clauses for Pakistani rental law and are admissible in court disputes.
Which payment methods are supported? ▼
We support JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and bank transfers. Both mobile wallet payments are instant and secure, making rent collection effortless for landlords and convenient for tenants without needing a bank account.
How is my trust score calculated? ▼
Your trust score is based on payment history (on-time rent), property care, communication responsiveness, and dispute resolution record. Build your score through positive behavior and reliable interactions. Both tenants and landlords have scores.
What happens in a dispute? ▼
Disputes are mediated through our AI system with expert arbitrators. We resolve conflicts fairly and quickly—typically within 3-5 days. All digital agreements and payment records serve as evidence, making resolution transparent and binding.
